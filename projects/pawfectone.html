MOODBOARD

The first step to the design process was creating a moodboard. I downloaded at least 5 different dating apps and took screenshots of their individual screens. From there, I compiled them into a master moodboard, collecting input from my teammates. Additionally, I researched possible colour palettes which aligned with our team's design goals. I also searched up cute mobile app inspiration. However, we would end up doing a website, but this still was useful in terms of content placement.

STAR CROSSED

Star Crossed is an upcoming and unique Bullet-Hell Dating sim currently in production since September 2023. It is an innovative fusion of Sci-Fi and Eldritch Horror, set in a mysterious post-apocalyptic planet. Our team consists of 15 highly passionate members, with a single individual leading each aspect of game production: Art, Programming, Design, Writing, and Music production.
